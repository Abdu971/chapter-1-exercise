Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy and indulgent Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. Perfect for a quick yet luxurious meal!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Remove from heat and quickly toss to combine.
  5. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
